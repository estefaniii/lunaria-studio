Our story

An agency with an entrepreneur's soul

Lunaria was born in La Chorrera, Panama, founded by Estéfani Torres, a strategic creative in love with detail and driven by the idea that design has to work — not just look pretty.

After watching so many small and mid-sized businesses pay a fortune for rigid agencies or, worse, hire freelancers without strategy who delivered generic work, we decided to create the space that was missing: an accessible premium digital agency, with serious methodology and human pricing.

Today we operate fully remotely, with clients across Latin America, the United States and other markets worldwide. Every project is treated as our own — we don't invoice and disappear, we stay to watch your brand grow with you.
